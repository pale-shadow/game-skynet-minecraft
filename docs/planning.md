# 🚀 Bitsmasher Network: AI Fields & Future Urbanization (2026-2027)

## 🏗️ The AI Fields Initiative
The primary goal of the upcoming development cycle is to expand the "Neon Frontier" beyond Shroomville into the **AI Fields**—a zone where world-building is managed by autonomous builders and procedural generation [1].

### 1. Autonomous Builder Expansion
*   **Infrastructure:** Finalize the migration of the secondary builder network to the **Raspberry Pi 5/Hailo** cluster to offload RCON building logic from the main server thread [1].
*   **Procedural Content:** Utilize the upgraded `decoration.py` regex-based parser to generate non-repeating "Void-Tech" structures, such as the **Computational Ender-Cores** and **Noodle Logic Cores** [2, 3].
*   **Player Observation:** Construct specialized observation decks at $Y:63$ and $Y:31$ to allow players to watch AI builders construct the **Deep-Rail** extensions in real-time [4, 5].

### 2. Player Engagement & Incentives
To entice players to leave the legacy hubs like **Washington Station**, we will implement the following:
*   **Void-Tech Scavenger Hunts:** Hidden loot caches within AI-generated structures like the **Sterile Growth Chambers**, utilizing the high-contrast **Tuff/Calcite/Froglight** palette [6, 7].
*   **Interactive AI Ambassadors:** Deploy **Citizens NPCs** within the AI Fields to explain the "Spore-to-Circuit" history and guide players through the **Deep-Rail** network [8-10].
*   **The "Noodle" Economy:** Launch the **Noodle Economy Exchange** to allow players to trade materials harvested from the "Industrial Spine" for unique AI-crafted rewards [11, 12].

### 3. Technical Roadmap
*   **Optimization:** Implement **Aikar’s Flags** on the Paper 1.21.11 instance to minimize garbage collection lag during massive AI-led chunk generation [13, 14].
*   **Pre-generation:** Use the **Chunky** plugin to pre-render the AI Fields within a 10,000-block radius to ensure smooth travel via Elytra [13].
*   **Security:** Enforce strict **LuckPerms** weights to protect legacy 2012 sites while granting "Hacker" and "Sheriff" ranks limited building permissions in the experimental AI zones [15, 16].
