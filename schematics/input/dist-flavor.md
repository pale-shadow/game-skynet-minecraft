📜 Dreamland District Flavor Text
1. The Shroomville Expansion (The Neon Frontier)
"Where nature meets high-velocity engineering. Shroomville is a bioluminescent sanctuary carved out of the original Mushroom Biome. Beneath its towering caps lies the heartbeat of modern Dreamland: the Deep-Rail Station. Whether you’re maintaining your fleet at the Western Repair Shed or seeking quiet reflection at the Cathedral Plaza, Shroomville represents our commitment to a sustainable, technical future."

2. Washington Station (The Zero Point)
"The birthplace of it all. Established in 2012, Washington Station has survived a dozen world-shaking updates and a decade of silence. It stands today as a monument to the 'Old Guard.' Its stone-and-oak architecture remains untouched, serving as the central hub of the legacy rail network and the primary gateway for all new Travelers entering Dreamland. Every cobblestone here has a story—if you know where to look."

3. The Noodle Factory & Industrial Spine
"The engine room of the economy. Situated along the Noodle-Business line, this massive industrial complex has provided the building blocks of our civilization for years. It is a place of loud pistons, sorting chests, and the smell of automated production. If you need it built, the Factory has the raw materials—just watch your step near the 2014 Chunk Glitch, a permanent scar on our world’s history."

4. The Maritime District (The Salt & The Sea)
"Docked eternally at the eastern edge of Shroomville, the SS Shroomville is a relic of the great migrations. Once a vessel of exploration, it now serves as our premier Maritime Museum and Coffee Shop. From the top of the local Lighthouse, you can see the edge of the world we’ve built—and the vast, unwritten ocean that still awaits the bold."
