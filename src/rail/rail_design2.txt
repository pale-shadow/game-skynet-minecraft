* [cite_start]**State Persistence:** Maintain `config/rail_registry.json` as a "digital twin," tracking switch UUIDs, coordinates, names, and current states[cite: 150].

#### 2. BlueMap POI Integration
* [cite_start]**Marker Logic:** The system must dynamically update BlueMap's `markers.json` file to reflect the live rail map[cite: 152].
* [cite_start]**Marker Type:** Use `poi` (Point of Interest) markers for rail switches[cite: 153].
* **Dynamic Styling:**

* [cite_start]**Update Frequency:** Markers are updated immediately upon successful RCON state change and hardware verification[cite: 156].
